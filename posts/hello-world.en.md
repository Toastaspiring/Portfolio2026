## Why a blog?

I've been building apps and other software for a while now, and I've realized one thing: **I learn better when I explain things to other people.** This blog is my space to do exactly that. (And no, it's not just to look serious on LinkedIn — though that helps too.)

On the menu:
- AI and computer vision projects
- Full-stack development patterns
- Debug sessions where I actually learned something
- The particularly weird problems I run into along the way
